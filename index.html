Who We Are

A Movement Born from Compassion

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Chhattisgarh.

The Foundation holds 80G & 12A certifications ensuring tax-exempt benefits for donors, is CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, listed with NITI Aayog, and holds the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification. With six flagship projects addressing hunger, education, animal welfare, women's empowerment, environment, and youth skill development — every rupee creates real, measurable impact.

Our Work

6 Flagship Projects

Each project targets a critical social need — together they form a complete ecosystem of change.

Humanitarian

Project SEVA

Hunger relief and social welfare — distributing food, clothing, and essential supplies to underprivileged communities across India.

🍽️ 50,000+ Meals Served
Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps by providing underprivileged children with school education support, digital literacy, and essential life skills.

🎒 Children Empowered Daily
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Dedicated to the rescue, protection, and daily care of stray animals. Volunteers ensure every street animal gets food, shelter, and medical attention.

🐕 50+ Animals Fed Daily
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Empowering rural women through self-help groups, skill development, financial independence coaching, and menstrual hygiene awareness programs.

💪 900+ Women Empowered
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Advocating environmental sustainability through large-scale tree plantation drives, eco-friendly agricultural practices, and conservation awareness.

🌳 20,000+ Saplings Planted
Youth Empowerment

Project VIKAS

Bridging education and employability through internships in digital marketing, finance, research, content writing, and data operations.

🎓 30,000+ Interns Trained
